Rabbit

A rabbit’s life span is about 8 years, though sterilized rabbits (those who are spayed/neutered) can live as long as 10-12 years.

  • Scientific Name:Oryctolagus
  • Average Length: 20-50 cm
  • Daily sleep: 8.4 hours
  • Habitat: Meadows,Forests, Grasslands.

A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is called a doe, and a male is a buck. A group of rabbits is called a herd.Happy rabbits practice a cute behavior known as a “binky:” they jump up in the air and twist and spin around.Rabbits are herbivores, eating a diet entirely of grasses and other plants.The average size of a rabbit litter is usually between 4 and 12 babies, which results after a short 30-day gestation. A rabbit’s teeth never stop growing.
